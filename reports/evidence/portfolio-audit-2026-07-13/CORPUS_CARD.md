# Sanitized public research corpus v1

Status: historical public-source snapshot, not a live collection

This corpus normalizes eight previously committed public-source observations
from two research backfills. It removes usernames, copied discussion text,
account identifiers, private notes, credentials, and raw exports. Observations
are short paraphrases tied to the original report, report checksum, capture date,
public URL, evidence role, and limitation.

The source pages were captured on 2026-05-23 or 2026-05-25 and were not
re-collected for this migration. URLs, products, and page contents can change;
the corpus must not be presented as current market research without a new dated
collection. The public URLs are retained for traceability, not as an endorsement.

## Allowed interpretation

- The file proves a privacy-safe, inspectable input shape for local Radar tests.
- It preserves existing-alternative and operator-problem observations with
  explicit limitations.
- It contains no human-recorded product decision, user feedback, adoption,
  revenue, production, or willingness-to-pay evidence.
- It does not complete the four-run or four-week longitudinal gate.

The corpus is not automatically consumed by live connectors and requires no
credentials. Any later execution must preserve its historical-capture label.
